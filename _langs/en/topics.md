
## Lecture Topics

Participants choose their own lecture topics, or alternatively, they can request a topic from their preferred instructor. In any case, the topic must be approved by organizers: Subham Roy ([subham.roy@matfyz.cuni.cz](mailto:subham.roy@matfyz.cuni.cz)) or Patrick Wynne ([patrick.wynne@matfyz.cuni.cz](mailto:patrick.wynne@matfyz.cuni.cz)). Please direct any questions about the lectures to them. Common topics include areas such as: algebra, geometry, logic, cryptography, and number theory.
